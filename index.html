About Me

Hello, I'm Arnav Chouksey, a highly accomplished flute player based in Apex, North Carolina. As the first chair flutist at Mills Park, I've delivered standout solo performances in pre-MPA concerts and showcased my talents before prestigious ensembles like the Green Level Band. My dedication to excellence has earned me selections in both the All-County Honors Band and All-District Honors Band, where I've honed my skills among the region's top young musicians. With a passion for mentoring, I'm committed to helping middle school students in my community elevate their flute playing, build strong fundamentals, and achieve their musical goals with confidence and precision.

Our Services

Personalized Video Sessions

One-on-one or small group guidance via Google Meets, tailored to your skill level for beginners, intermediates, and advanced students.

Audition Preparation

Specialized help for All-County and All-District auditions, including sight-reading practice, solo repertoire, and performance techniques.

Fundamentals Mastery

Build a solid foundation with lessons on posture, breathing, fingerings, and note accuracy for beginners starting their flute journey.

Repertoire and Band Support

Work through pieces of music, school band repertoire, scales, and dynamics to help intermediates and advanced players refine their skills and tackle challenging material.

Flute Tutorials

Getting Started with the Flute

Essential basics for beginners: assembling your flute, proper posture, breathing techniques, and playing your first notes. Ideal for 6th graders new to the instrument.

Mastering Basic Scales and Fingerings

Step-by-step practice on major and minor scales, with tips for smooth finger transitions and building speed for 7th graders strengthening their fundamentals.

Sight-Reading and Audition Tips

Strategies for effective sight-reading, common pitfalls to avoid, and preparation for auditions like All-County and All-District for 8th graders aiming high.

Working Through Repertoire

Guidance on interpreting pieces of music, dynamics, articulation, and integrating with school band music to enhance overall performance.

Note: These tutorials are free resources. For personalized video sessions or feedback, reach out via email.
