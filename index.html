About Me

Hey there! I'm Arnav Chouksey, an passionate and accomplished flute player from Apex, North Carolina. As the first chair flutist at Mills Park, I've thrilled audiences with standout solo performances in pre-MPA concerts and shared the stage with amazing groups like the Green Level Band. My hard work has led me to selections in both the All-County Honors Band and All-District Honors Band, where I've grown alongside the best young talents in the region. Now, I'm super excited to pass on that passion and knowledge to you! Whether you're just starting out or aiming for the stars, I'm here to cheer you on, help you build unbreakable fundamentals, and watch you achieve incredible musical goals with joy and confidence. Let's make beautiful music together!

Our Services

Personalized Video Sessions

You're amazing, and I'm here to prove it! Join me for fun, one-on-one or small group sessions via Google Meets, customized just for you—whether you're a beginner, intermediate, or advanced. We'll celebrate every step forward!

Audition Preparation

Believe in yourself—you can nail those auditions! Get pumped with specialized support for All-County and All-District, including sight-reading fun, solo repertoire mastery, and techniques to make you stand out and feel unstoppable.

Fundamentals Mastery

Flute Tutorials

Getting Started with the Flute

You're off to a fantastic start! Discover essential basics like assembling your flute, perfect posture, breathing techniques, and playing your first notes. This is tailored for 6th graders ready to embark on an exciting adventure— you've got this!

Mastering Basic Scales and Fingerings

Keep going strong—you're building something awesome! Enjoy step-by-step practice on major and minor scales, with helpful tips for smooth finger transitions and speed-building, ideal for 7th graders powering up their fundamentals.

Sight-Reading and Audition Tips

You're capable of amazing things! Learn winning strategies for sight-reading, dodging common pitfalls, and gearing up for auditions like All-County and All-District—perfect for 8th graders chasing their dreams with confidence.

Working Through Repertoire

Shine bright—you're a star in the making! Get guidance on interpreting pieces of music, mastering dynamics and articulation, and blending it all with your school band music to boost your performance to new heights.

Note: These tutorials are free and full of encouragement to help you grow. For personalized video sessions or extra cheers, just email me—I'm rooting for you!
