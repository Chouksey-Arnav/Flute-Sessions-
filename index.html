About Me

Hi, I'm Arnav Chouksey — first chair flutist at Mills Park in Apex, North Carolina. I started exactly where a lot of you are right now. In sixth grade first quarter I was ready to quit band because the squeaks were driving me crazy. But I loved how the flute looked and the way a clean note sounded when I finally got it right, so I stuck with it. That choice changed my life.

By seventh grade I was sitting 10th chair, but I used that as fuel. I practiced smarter. Then in eighth grade I locked in over summer break with the same hacks I'm giving you today. The result? First chair at Mills Park, pre-MPA soloist in front of the Green Level Band, All-County and All-District honors band member, and an invitation to the NC Bandmasters trip to Europe.

I'm not some distant teacher — I'm a middle-schooler who just went through everything you're going through. That's why everything here is 100% free and built for you.

Why I'm Doing This

I remember how lonely and frustrating it felt when I almost quit in sixth grade. No one showed me the simple tricks that would have made everything click faster. I don't want any of you to feel that way. That's why I created Arnav Chouksey Flute Academy — completely free, no catch, just real help from someone who was exactly in your shoes.

If I can help even one kid fall in love with the flute the way I did, or make it to All-County, or get invited on a trip to Europe, then every minute I spend here is worth it. This isn't just about playing notes — it's about giving you the confidence and skills that open doors you didn't even know existed.
