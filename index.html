Summer Lock-In

The Plan That Changed Everything.

10th of 16 → First Chair → All-County → Europe. One summer. Four rules.

01

Daily Practice — No Exceptions. ⏰

Phone away. Full presence. Consistent beats long every single time.

🌟 5th: 10 min — 5 breath · 5 tone
💚 6th: 15 min — 5 tone · 5 notes · 5 worst spot
💙 7th: 20 min — 5 scales · 5 sight-read · 5 piece · 5 weakness
⭐ 8th: 20 min — 5 long tones · 5 scales · 5 piece · 5 artistry
02

Drill Your Worst Spot First. 🎯

Practice what's comfortable and you'll stay comfortable. One worst spot per week — every session, until it stops being scary.

03

Record Every Session. 📱

Your brain fills in the sound you expect, not what's coming out. Record. Listen like a judge. Fix it. Repeat.

04

Listen to the Best Daily. 🎧

Galway. Pahud. Jasmine Choi. Intentional listening, every day. Your ears need a target before your hands can aim.
