About Me

Hey friend, I'm Arnav Chouksey — first-chair flutist at Mills Park, soloist in pre-MPA concerts, and a proud member of both All-County and All-District Honors Bands. I've stood on stage in front of the Green Level Band and delivered performances that earned respect from directors and peers alike.

But here's what matters most to me: I remember exactly how it felt to be a middle-schooler learning flute — the nerves, the squeaks, the excitement when something finally clicked. That's why I'm here. With the same dedication that got me to honors bands, I'll guide you logically from your very first note all the way to audition-ready solos. Most importantly, I'll be your biggest cheerleader, celebrating every win until you feel unstoppable and completely in love with the flute.

Services Built for Your Success

Personalized Video Sessions

One-on-one or small-group Google Meets tailored exactly to your level. Beginners get patient foundations, intermediates get polished technique, and advanced players get audition-level mastery — because every student deserves teaching that actually fits them.

All-County & All-District Audition Prep

Proven strategies that have already helped students make honors bands. Sight-reading drills, solo repertoire deep-dives, tone work, and performance psychology — everything you need to walk into auditions confident and prepared.

Fundamentals Mastery

Rock-solid basics (posture, breathing, fingerings, tone) taught in fun, bite-sized steps. Perfect for 6th graders just starting — or anyone who wants to finally fix those squeaks for good.

Repertoire & Band Music Support

We'll play through your school pieces, concert repertoire, scales, and dynamics together. Whether it's helping you sound amazing in band class or preparing a solo that wows the judges — I've got you.
