Summer Lock-In

The Plan That Changed Everything.

10th of 16 → First Chair → All-County → All-District → Europe. One summer. Four rules. Here they are.

01

Daily Practice — Every. Single. Day. ⏰

No exceptions. Weekends. Vacation days. Days you don't feel like it. Phone in another room. Full presence. Consistent beats long every time.

🌟 5th: 10 min — 5 breath · 5 headjoint tone
💚 6th: 15 min — 5 tone · 5 notes · 5 worst spot
💙 7th: 20 min — 5 scales · 5 sight-read · 5 piece · 5 weakness
⭐ 8th: 20 min — 5 long tones · 5 scales · 5 piece · 5 artistry
02

Attack Your Worst Spot First. 🎯

Most students practice what feels comfortable. Real growth lives in the uncomfortable places. One worst spot per week. Drill it first, every session. Stack the wins.

03

Record Yourself Every Session. 📱

Your brain lies to you while you play. It fills in the sound you expect, not what's actually coming out. Record. Listen back like a judge. Fix it. Repeat. This alone changed everything for me.

04

Listen to the Best Players Daily. 🎧

Every day that summer — Galway, Pahud, Jasmine Choi. Not background music. Intentional listening. Your ears need a clear target before your hands can aim at one.
