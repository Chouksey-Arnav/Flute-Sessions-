About Me

Hi, I'm Arnav Choksey, a passionate flute player based in Apex, North Carolina. With years of experience playing the flute, I'm dedicated to helping young students in my community discover the joy of music. I offer free tutorials and personalized guidance to 6th, 7th, and 8th graders to help them improve their skills, prepare for band classes, and build confidence in their musical abilities.
